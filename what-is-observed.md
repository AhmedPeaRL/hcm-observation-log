## What May Be Observed

The following events may be logged:

- The appearance of an artifact (identifier + timestamp only)
- A visit or access event
- A download or acquisition
- A payment occurrence
- A failed publication attempt
- The disappearance or unavailability of a channel

All observations must be:

- Descriptive, not evaluative
- Atomic, not aggregated
- Free of comparison or interpretation

Example (allowed):
"Artifact_003 appeared at 2025-03-21T10:14Z"

Example (not allowed):
"Artifact_003 performed better than previous artifacts"
